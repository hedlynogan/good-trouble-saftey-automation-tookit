# Legal Rights Audio Script - English
# Each line will be converted to a separate audio segment

# Introduction
This is a safety reminder about your legal rights.

# Core Rights
You have the right to remain silent.

You have the right to refuse to answer questions.

You have the right to ask if you are free to leave.

You have the right to a lawyer.

You do not have to consent to a search.

You do not have to sign anything without a lawyer.

# Safety Reminders
Stay calm and respectful.

Keep your hands visible.

Do not resist or argue.

Ask for badge numbers and names.
